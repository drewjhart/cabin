A Cabin in the Woods
A friend of mine was looking for a tiny home in which he could live semi-nomadically. As such, the plan of the house was sized to align with an oversized transportation permit. Program includes space for a kitchen, a small living area, storage and a bed in the loft.

This is built on three.js and imports Rhino geometry.
